Command Reference

Open Tabris with Ctrl + K (Windows/Linux) or ⌘ + K (Mac), then type a command. Here are some examples:

Navigation

ft - Find a tab

Organization

gt - Auto group tabs (by domain or time)
ug - Ungroup tabs
mc - Move tab to group

This is a subset of available commands. Explore more commands directly in Tabris!
